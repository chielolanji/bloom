🌷 For You

Hi, Aki. I'm not as poetic as you, and I'm not as impressive either. My words may never be as beautiful as yours, but I hope you'll accept them anyway. Just like these tulips, you're beautiful in your own way. They bloom with vibrant colors, much like the color you've brought into my life. You brightened my weary blues and reminded me that even the dullest days can bloom into something beautiful. So these flowers are for you—a small reflection of the beauty and warmth you've shared with me.
